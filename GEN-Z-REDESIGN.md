## 🚀 How to Customize Further

### Add Your Video Demo
In the video section, replace:
```html
<div class="video-placeholder">
  📱 demo video placeholder
</div>
```

With:
```html
<video controls>
  <source src="./public/app-demo.mp4" type="video/mp4">
</video>
```

### Update WhatsApp Messages
Change the screenshots in the "WHATSAPP PROOF" section to match actual reviews if you have them

### Adjust Copy
All the Gen Z language is in the HTML. Search and replace as needed. Examples:
- "if this is you..." - change scenarios
- Test card text - customize if needed
- Hero tagline - make it your own
