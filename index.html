Questions

You got questions. We got answers.

Is this an official Bahria University app? Will I get in trouble?
Nah, BU Buddy is not affiliated with Bahria University. We're just Bahria University students who got tired of the portal. We scrape public data (same data you see when you login) and present it better. It's like using a better calculator for math homework—not cheating, just smarter.
How do you access my attendance without my password?
Magic. Just kidding—you enter your credentials once, we encrypt them on your device (AES-256), and use them to fetch your data. We literally cannot see your password. It's stored in your phone's secure storage, not our servers. We're too lazy to steal your data anyway.
What if BU Buddy stops working?
Then you go back to suffering with the Bahria University portal. But seriously, if Bahria University changes their system, we'll update within 24-48 hours. We've been running for 6 months without issues. Plus, it's free during beta, so you literally have nothing to lose except 2 minutes of download time.
Can I trust you with my data?
We don't store your data on our servers. Everything stays on your device. We couldn't sell your data even if we wanted to (we don't). We're students, not Facebook. Your attendance percentage isn't valuable to advertisers anyway.
iOS or Android?
Android first (APK download), iOS coming soon™. We know, we know, iPhone users suffer too. We're working on it. Join the waitlist and we'll email you the second it's ready. Probably. If we remember.
Why is this free?
Because we're nice. Also, we need users to test it. Also also, we might charge later once we add more features. Get in now while it's free and you'll keep it free forever. Early bird gets the worm and all that.
